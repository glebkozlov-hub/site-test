Advanced Material Technology

Materials

Standard constructions include linen, aramid, and linen/polyester. The material is selected based on required wear behavior, dimensional stability, and handling characteristics, as well as the specific conditions of the machine and product.

Coating options

Available in three surface configurations: Uncoated, Center Coated (working zone), and Full Coated. Coating is chosen to match friction behavior and suction interaction in operation, helping maintain stable tracking and consistent performance under your line conditions.

Advanced weaving

Controlled weave construction supports dimensional stability and consistent behavior over the tape's service interval. Weaving parameters are aligned with the selected material and coating to ensure repeatable handling and predictable operation in daily production.
